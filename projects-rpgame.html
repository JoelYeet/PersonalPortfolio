RPGame

Step into the shoes of a vigilante amidst a city plagued by crime. Embark on quests to boost your respect within the underworld, unlocking access to a vast array of powerful weapons and gear by collecting the spoils from defeated foes!

Genre: Shooter, Role Playing
‍
Tools Used: Unity Engine, Microsoft Excel

RPGame is a top-down action game that incorporates light role-playing mechanics, conceived during a system design module.


Developed collaboratively with a close friend, this project pays homage to the iconic pre-3D era of Grand Theft Auto games. Drawing inspiration from the gameplay dynamics and nostalgic charm of those classics, RPGame combines engaging action elements with RPG elements to create a unique experience.RPGame adheres strictly to the constraints and specifications outlined in the module, focusing entirely on being systems-driven. Every aspect of the game, from its core mechanics to the intricate data within each system, is meticulously balanced using precise mathematical formulas.
