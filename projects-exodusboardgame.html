EXODUS Board Game

Exodus: The Board Game is a conceptual adaptation of the story of Exodus into a board game format.

Genre: Board Game

Tools Used: Adobe Creative Suite

Drawing inspiration from a previous video game project, I wanted to explore the possibility of adapting it into a board game format. I aimed to portray a gritty and dark future, marking a shift from the previous theme, Drawing inspiration from iconic sci-fi action films like Independence Day and Battle: Los Angeles, the concept seeks to capture the intensity and scale of alien invasions, the resilience of humanity in the face of overwhelming odds, and the chaos of large-scale battles.

These themes are reflected in the design elements, including the militaristic iconography and imagery featured on the logo, box cover, playing mat, and individual cards.

Each component is designed to capture the intensity of large-scale battles, the grounded and tactical nature of human military forces and equipment, and the ominous presence of an alien threat. Together, these elements create an immersive and gritty, high-stakes atmosphere for players.

A combination striking visuals, compelling themes, and immersive design to transport players into a dark, militaristic future where survival hinges on strategy and resilience against overwhelming alien forces. promising an engaging experience that captures the tension and thrill of humanity‚Äôs fight for survival.
