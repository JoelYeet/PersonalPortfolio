LINE

LINE (Luminous Intelligence Navigation Enterprise) is a 4 player card game about racing to the centre of the map. Four players play on a 13x13 tile grid with four starting spaces on the middle of each edge. The objective of the game is to place cards and move their token to the middle of the grid first or obstruct other players.

Genre: Card Game

Tools Used: Adobe Creative Suite

LINE emerged as a product of my participation in a design module during my inaugural year at university. This endeavour marked my initial venture into the realm of card game development, which turned out to be an immensely enlightening experience.
